Key Attractions

Alnwick Castle

Harry Potter Filming Location

The iconic Hogwarts exterior from the first two Harry Potter films. Experience broomstick training and explore the medieval castle.

3 hours Pre-book online

Hadrian's Wall

UNESCO World Heritage Site

Walk along the ancient Roman wall at Housesteads Fort, the best-preserved section with spectacular countryside views.

2.5 hours 750m uphill walk

York Minster

Gothic Cathedral

One of the world's most magnificent cathedrals with breathtaking medieval architecture and stunning stained glass windows.

2 hours Tower climb available

Lindisfarne (Holy Island)

Tidal Island

Historic monastery ruins and castle on a tidal island. Access depends on tide times - plan carefully!

3 hours Tide dependent

JORVIK Viking Centre

Archaeological Experience

Journey through 1,000-year-old Viking streets built on the actual archaeological site where Vikings once lived.

1.5 hours Pre-book: 01904 615505

The Shambles

Medieval Street

One of Europe's best-preserved medieval shopping streets with overhanging timber buildings and Harry Potter merchandise.

1 hour Free to explore
