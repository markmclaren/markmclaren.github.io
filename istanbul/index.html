Essential Tips

üê±
Cat Etiquette

Istanbul's street cats are friendly but respect their space. They're well-fed by locals, but appreciate gentle pets. Don't feed cats in restaurants or mosques without permission.

üí∞
Money Matters

Turkish Lira (TL) is the currency. Credit cards widely accepted, but carry cash for small shops and street food. ATMs available throughout Sultanahmet.

ü§ù
Bargaining

Expected at bazaars and for football shirts. Start at 40-50% of asking price. Be friendly and patient. Walk away if price is too high‚Äîthey'll often call you back!

üïå
Dress Code

Modest dress for mosques (shoulders and knees covered). Headscarves provided for women at mosque entrances. Remove shoes before entering mosques.

‚öΩ Football Shirt Shopping Pro Tips

The shops just outside the Spice Bazaar (Egyptian Bazaar) are known for high-quality replica jerseys. Shop on Saturday since Grand Bazaar is closed Sundays!

Expected Prices:

  • Good quality replicas: 200-400 TL (¬£6-12)
  • Lower quality: 100-200 TL (¬£3-6)

Bargaining Tips:

  • Start at 30-40% of asking price
  • Check stitching and logos carefully
  • Ask "En iyi fiyat nedir?" (Best price?)
