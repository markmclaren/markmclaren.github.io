Practical Information

Everything you need to know for a smooth family adventure

Getting Around

  • First Bus: Children under 16 travel FREE during summer holidays
  • Gromit App: Download for sculpture locations and rewards
  • Parking: Free at Puxton Park, paid in Bristol city center
  • Accessibility: All locations have accessible parking spaces

Family Dining (Lunch Options)

  • Five The Beach: Seafront dining with outdoor terrace (Clevedon)
  • The Athenian: Bristol harbourside location with family menu
  • Puxton Park: On-site restaurant and farm shop
  • All venues: High chairs and accessible facilities available

Special Needs Support

  • Puxton Park: Free carer tickets, sensory-friendly environment
  • Clevedon: Level promenade, quiet spaces available
  • Bristol: Accessible transport, changing facilities
  • All locations: Staff trained to assist families with additional needs
